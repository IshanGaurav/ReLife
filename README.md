# Amazon HackOn Season 6.0 | Team Alpha

### Giving Every Product a Meaningful Second Life

Amazon ReLife is an **end-to-end circular commerce solution** that helps returned, underused, and pre-owned products find their next best destination through resale, refurbishment, donation, and recycling.

Instead of allowing valuable products to become waste, Amazon ReLife creates a trusted ecosystem where customers, sellers, and Amazon all benefit from extending product lifecycles.

---

# Why This Matters

Every year, millions of products purchased through e-commerce are returned, underused, or discarded despite remaining functional.

This creates challenges for everyone in the ecosystem:

* Customers hesitate to purchase second-hand products because they cannot verify quality or authenticity.
* Sellers struggle to recover value from returned inventory.
* Functional products lose value and eventually become waste.
* Buyers miss affordable alternatives that could meet their needs.
* Product returns increase logistics costs and environmental impact.

Amazon ReLife addresses these challenges by creating a trusted circular commerce experience within Amazon and delivering an end-to-end solution from product acquisition to product reuse.

# End-to-End Product Lifecycle

```text
Customer Purchases Product
            ↓
      Uses Product
            ↓
 Lists Product on ReLife
            ↓
 AI Verification Engine
            ↓
 Digital Passport Created
            ↓
 Verified Marketplace Listing
            ↓
 New Buyer Purchases
            ↓

```

This lifecycle forms the foundation of the Amazon ReLife end-to-end solution.
